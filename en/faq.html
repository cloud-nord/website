Frequently Asked Questions

Why is 2020 edition of Devfest Lille is canceled ?

Given the period that we are currently going through, linked to the covid-19 epidemic, it is with regret that we had to cancel the Devfest Lille 2020 which should have taken place on June 12th.

Last speech of french président Emmanuel Macron banning gatherings until mid July only confirmed our concerns on our ability to organize an event in good sanitary conditions.

Unfortunately, faced with the uncertainty regarding the deconfinement procedures and the time required before a return to normal, we made the decision to cancel the Devfest Lille 2020.

How to get a refund for your 2020 edition ticket?

The refund will be made automatically by the end of May to the bank account that purchased the ticket.

What is the Devfest?

The Devfests are large scale events around the world, following the tradition of the Google Developer Days and organized by groups of local users. These events are an unique opportunity to share knowledge and experiences about the web, the cloud, mobile.

The Devfest Lille will be followed by an after-party that give you a friendly occasion to meet new people and do some networking.

The talks and agenda will be set by the beginning of April 2021, after the review of our CFP submissions.

Who organizes the event?

The DevFest Lille is organized by the GDG Lille, a group of 10 voluntary developers, passionate by new technologies.

How can I become a partner?

You may consult our Partnership Agreement, and contact us by email at the address contact@gdglille.org, specifying the pack you are interested in.

What can I expect from Devfest Lille?

You will surely have an amazing day of conferences (50mn) — including awesome keynotes — and quickies (20mn), presented by experient speakers or newbies.
